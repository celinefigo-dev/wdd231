Site Plan: Clothe a Child Initiative

Site Name

Clothe a Child Initiative

This is a nonprofit website dedicated to collecting new and preloved clothes and distributing them to underprivileged children.>

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to raise awareness of the clothing needs of underprivileged children and encourage people to donate clothes or financial support. It will also serve as a platform for volunteers and donors to get involved in making a difference..

Scenarios

  1. Scenario 1: "A mother has bags of outgrown clothes and wants to know how she can donate them through the website."
  2. Scenario 2: "A company wants to sponsor a clothing drive and needs contact details for collaboration."
