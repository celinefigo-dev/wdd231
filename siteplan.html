Our Mission

The Clothe a Child Initiative is a non-profit project dedicated to collecting and distributing new and preloved clothes to underprivileged children. We believe that every child deserves to feel confident, cared for, and comfortable in their clothing. With your help, we make that possible.

How to Donate

We accept gently used and new children's clothing. Please make sure items are clean and sorted by age and size. Drop-off or pickup arrangements can be made by contacting us.
